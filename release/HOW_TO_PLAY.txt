Survive2Sunrise - How to play (Windows)
========================================

1. Extract this entire folder if you downloaded a zip file.
2. Double-click PLAY.bat (not console_fnaf.exe directly).
3. A black game window will open. Keep that window focused.

Controls
--------
  A  -  Toggle left door
  D  -  Toggle right door
  Q  -  Quit

The animatronics
-----------------
Freddo and Chico used to headline the pizzeria stage—Freddo on vocals, Chico on
the side acts. After hours they still roam the building. Freddo works the western
halls toward your left door; Chico circles the east and kitchens toward your right.
Hold the matching door shut long enough and they'll back off, but every closed door
drains your battery faster. As the night wears on, they grow bolder.

Goal
----
Survive until sunrise without running out of battery or letting an animatronic in.

Troubleshooting
---------------
- If Windows SmartScreen warns about an unknown app, choose "More info" then "Run anyway"
  (the game is not code-signed).
- Always start the game with PLAY.bat so the terminal libraries load correctly.
- If the window closes immediately, make sure all files stayed in the same folder after extract.

On a Mac? Download console-fnaf-macos.zip instead and double-click Play.command.
See HOW_TO_PLAY_mac.txt in that zip.
